Brief overview of the destination

  1. Kinkaku-ji (Golden Pavilion) - A stunning Zen temple covered in gold leaf.
  2. Fushimi Inari Taisha - Famous for its thousands of vibrant orange torii gates.
  3. Arashiyama Bamboo Grove - A serene and picturesque bamboo forest.
  4. Gion District - The historic geisha district with charming wooden machiya houses.
  5. Kiyomizu-dera Temple - A UNESCO World Heritage site with panoramic city views.
  • Kaiseki - A traditional multi-course Japanese meal known for its artistry and flavors.
  • Matcha - High-quality powdered green tea used in tea ceremonies and desserts.
  • Yudofu - A simple yet flavorful boiled tofu dish.
  • Sushi - Fresh and delicate, Kyoto-style sushi is a must-try.
  • Yatsuhashi - A sweet treat made from cinnamon-flavored rice flour.
